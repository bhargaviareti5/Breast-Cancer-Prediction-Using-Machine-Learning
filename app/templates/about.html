About Our Research Initiative

Pioneering the integration of artificial intelligence and wearable sensing technologies to transform the management of chronic diseases like breast cancer.

Our Mission & Vision

At the forefront of digital health innovation, our research initiative aims to bridge the gap between cutting-edge technology and practical healthcare solutions for chronic disease management.

We envision a future where continuous, non-invasive monitoring powered by artificial intelligence enables early detection, personalized treatment, and improved quality of life for millions living with breast cancer.

By combining expertise in biomedical engineering, data science, and clinical medicine, we're developing wearable sensing technologies that provide real-time insights and predictive analytics for better health outcomes.

About Our Research
